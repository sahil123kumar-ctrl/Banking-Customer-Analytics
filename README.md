# Banking Customer Analytics

## Overview
The **Banking Customer Analytics Project** provides an in-depth analysis of a bank’s customer behavior, transaction patterns, branch performance, and employee operations using **SQL** and **Power BI**.  
It helps financial institutions understand customer engagement, identify high-value clients, detect dormant accounts, and optimize branch and employee performance.

---

## Objectives
1. Analyze customer demographics and transaction behavior.  
2. Identify high-value and inactive customers.  
3. Evaluate the performance of branches and employees.  
4. Detect seasonal and regional transaction trends.  
5. Support data-driven decision-making for marketing and operations.

---

## Dataset Overview
The project uses five main datasets:

1. **Customers** — Customer demographic and contact information.  
2. **Accounts** — Account types, balances, and branch associations.  
3. **Transactions** — Deposits, withdrawals, transfers, and timestamps.  
4. **Branches** — Branch details, city, and performance indicators.  
5. **Employees** — Staff roles, joining dates, and branch assignments.

---

## Tools & Technologies
1. **SQL** — For data extraction, cleaning, and aggregation.  
2. **Power BI** — For data visualization and interactive dashboards.  
3. **Excel** — For pre-analysis and data validation.

## Problem Statements

### 1. Customer Analytics
1. How many customers are there in each city?  
2. Which account type has the highest average balance?  
3. Who are the top 5 customers with the highest account balances?  
4. Which customer holds the highest balance in each branch?  
5. Which customers have high balances but low transaction activity?  
6. Which customers frequently make small withdrawals?  
7. Which customers have deposited the highest total amount of money?  

---

### 2. Transaction Insights
1. What is the total transaction count and amount by transaction type?  
2. Which customers have the highest transaction volumes?  
3. What is the monthly trend of total transactions?  
4. Which are the top three months with the highest transaction amounts?  
5. What are the top three highest transactions per branch?  
6. What is the monthly growth rate of transactions?  
7. What is the total transaction count and amount per account per month?  
8. What is the total transaction amount performed by each customer group?  

---

### 3. Branch Insights
1. Which branches have the highest total balance across accounts?  
2. How many customers are associated with each branch?  
3. How many employees work in each branch?  
4. Which branches have the highest transaction volume?  
5. Which branch has the highest average account balance?  

---

### 4. Employee Insights
1. How many employees work in each branch?  
2. Which branches have the highest staff count and performance levels?  
3. Which roles (e.g., cashiers, advisors) contribute most to customer satisfaction?  
4. How does employee experience affect branch performance?  

---

### 5. Advanced Functional Insights
1. What is the moving average of transactions over the last three months?  
2. What is the customer lifetime proxy?  
3. Which accounts have more than five transactions per day?  
4. What is the average number of transactions per customer per month over the last year?  
5. Which customers have an average transaction amount higher than the overall bank average?  
6. How are branches ranked based on total deposits in the last quarter?

## Key Insights

### Customer Insights
1. **Mumbai** has the highest number of customers; **Mysuru** the least — showing strong and weak regional markets.  
2. **Savings Accounts** hold the highest average balance, contributing significantly to bank revenue.  
3. Top customers (e.g., *Vardaniya Tata*) hold major deposits and require personalized relationship management.  
4. Wealthy but inactive customers (e.g., *Ojas Kari*) present untapped engagement potential.  

### Transaction Insights
1. **Transfers dominate** all transaction types, reflecting the rise of digital banking.  
2. **May 2024** recorded the highest transaction activity due to salary bonuses and festive spending.  
3. **Ira Sur** is the most active customer by transaction volume.  

### Branch Insights
1. **Indus Silicon Valley Branch** leads in both total balance and transaction volume.  
2. **KMB Hitech City Branch** has the largest workforce, showing operational strength.  
3. Some branches show high customer activity but limited staff, indicating potential overwork.  

### Employee Insights
1. Senior employees contribute strongly to customer satisfaction and operational stability.  
2. Branches with new joiners require better training and mentoring programs.  
3. Cashiers form the largest employee group, reflecting heavy in-person service demand.  

### Cross-Functional Insights
1. **High-balance, low-activity** customers are profitable but under-engaged — ideal for reactivation campaigns.  
2. **High-transaction, high-balance** customers represent loyal long-term clients.  
3. Unusual transaction spikes may indicate potential **fraud or misuse**.

## Recommendations

### 1. Strengthen Customer Engagement
1. Re-engage inactive high-balance customers through loyalty programs or personalized offers.  
2. Offer cashback and seasonal bonuses to increase transaction activity.  

### 2. Personalize Banking Services
1. Use customer segmentation analytics to tailor products like premium savings or retirement plans.  
2. Provide premium support and exclusive benefits to high-value clients.  

### 3. Boost Digital Banking
1. Promote online and mobile banking to reduce branch congestion.  
2. Educate older customers on digital platforms using training and incentives.  

### 4. Optimize Branch Operations
1. Recognize and reward top-performing branches such as *Indus Silicon Valley*.  
2. Reallocate employees based on workload and customer volume.  
3. Investigate low-performing branches for local or operational challenges.  

### 5. Enhance Cross-Selling
1. Target high-transaction customers for additional products (credit cards, insurance, investments).  
2. Bundle services to improve customer retention and satisfaction.  

### 6. Strengthen Risk and Fraud Detection
1. Monitor repetitive or abnormal transactions to identify potential fraud.  
2. Implement real-time analytics alerts for unusual transaction activity.  

### 7. Expand Market Presence
1. Focus marketing on underrepresented cities and low-customer regions.  
2. Introduce **digital-only branches** to reduce costs and reach tech-savvy customers.
